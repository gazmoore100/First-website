Welcome to Elite Human Performance

Manchester's Leading Strength and Conditioning Specialists





Hello and welcome to Elite Human Performance. I'm Gary, a REPs certified level 3 CYQ personal trainer, VTCT sports massage therapist and British weightlifting level 1 coach with over 10 years experience working in the fitness industry. I'm currently studying for a BSc (Hons) in sports science (strength&conditioning) to further my knowledge in the field of sports science and to gain professional accreditation as a fully qualified strength and conditioning coach. I specialise in sports specific training, strength&conditioning and olympic lifting. I also provide one to one personal training services for all ages and abilities for those looking to improve their health and physique. As a personal trainer I have worked with a diverse group of people including amateur and semi professional athletes including those with physical and mental disabilities. Sport has always been and remains a major part of my lifestyle. Specifically, I am a keen rugby union player, spending 4 years at the Leicester Tigers Academy, representing Leicestershire and the Midlands at various age groups as well as gaining national league playing experience with Burnage RFC.

Certifications
1st Class Honors Degree in Sports Science. Level 3 Advanced Personal Trainer. Diploma in Sports & Exercise science. VTCT Level 3 Sports Massage therapy. British weightlifting Level 1. Level 3 Nutrition and weight management.
